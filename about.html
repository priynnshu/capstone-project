About MedLink

MedLink is a student-built healthcare platform that aims to provide accessible health information and easy doctor connections. Our goal is to make healthcare simple, transparent, and available for everyone.

We believe that good health is the foundation of a happy life. Our mission is to bring quality health support through technology and user-friendly design.
